# PROJECT DIRECTIVE
## JCI BOHOLANA KISSES – HIGH-IMPACT ORGANIZATION WEBSITE

---

# 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Design and structure a professional, high-impact website for:

Organization Name: JCI Boholana Kisses  
Category: Community Organization  
Identity: All women–LGBTQ++ chapter of JCI Philippines  
Location: Tagbilaran City, Philippines  

The website must communicate:

- Leadership
- Inclusivity
- Professionalism
- Community impact
- Credibility

The website is NOT decorative. It must be structured, mission-driven, and conversion-focused.

---

# 2. CORE OBJECTIVE

The website must:

- Inspire trust
- Clearly communicate mission and identity
- Showcase key projects
- Encourage membership and partnerships
- Maintain consistent branding

Primary actions to encourage:
- Join the organization
- View projects
- Partner or collaborate
- Contact the organization

---

# 3. BRAND IDENTITY RULES

## Color System

Primary:
- JCI Maroon (#8C0000)

Accent:
- Gold (for highlights and premium feel)
- Blue (for trust sections)
- Pink (subtle inclusivity accent only)

Rules:
- Do NOT overuse pink.
- Maintain strong contrast and accessibility.
- Buttons must be consistent in style and color.

---

## Typography Rules

- Use clean, modern sans-serif typography.
- Large bold headlines.
- Clear hierarchy:
  - H1: Strong mission-driven statements
  - H2: Section titles
  - H3: Card titles
  - Body: Short, readable paragraphs
- Avoid long text blocks.

---

# 4. HOMEPAGE STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)

## SECTION 1 – HERO

Must include:
- Strong emotional headline (NOT “Welcome”)
- Short mission-driven subtext (1–2 lines)
- Two CTA buttons:
  - Join Us
  - View Projects

Design:
- Clean layout
- Clear spacing
- Immediate impact

---

## SECTION 2 – ABOUT

Content focus:
- Who they are
- All women–LGBTQ++ chapter identity
- Leadership development
- Community service
- Advocacy focus

Rules:
- Maximum 3–4 short paragraphs
- Keep messaging powerful and concise

---

## SECTION 3 – FEATURED PROJECTS

Display as card grid layout.

Must include:
- JCI Discover & JCI Explore
- GABAY 2026 – Visayas Year 5
- Love is Not Blind (VAWC Webinar)
- Hearts in Bloom

Each card must contain:
- Project title
- Short 2–3 line description
- Optional “Learn More” button

---

## SECTION 4 – IMPACT / SOCIAL PROOF

Highlight:
- 5.7K+ Followers
- Active since 2010
- Community initiatives
- Visayas leadership participation

Use visual emphasis:
- Counters or bold statistics
- Clear alignment
- Strong spacing

---

## SECTION 5 – EVENTS / NEWS

Layout:
- Card or timeline format
- Clean and organized
- Professional tone

---

## SECTION 6 – STRONG CALL TO ACTION

Full-width banner section.

Headline example tone:
“Be Part of the Movement.”

Include buttons:
- Become a Member
- Partner With Us
- Contact Us

Must visually stand out from previous sections.

---

## SECTION 7 – FOOTER

Include:
- Address:
  17 - E Carlos P. Garcia Avenue, Tagbilaran City

  jciboholanakisses2010@gmail.com
- Affiliation mention with JCI Philippines
- Social links

Footer must look structured and professional.

---

# 5. DESIGN CONSISTENCY RULES

- Maintain consistent button styles.
- Maintain equal spacing between sections.
- Align content to grid system.
- Avoid clutter.
- Avoid inconsistent font sizes.
- Avoid random color usage.
- White space is required.
- Professional tone at all times.

---

# 6. CONTENT WRITING RULES

Headlines must:
- Be mission-driven
- Be empowering
- Avoid generic phrases

Avoid:
- “Welcome to our website”
- Long paragraphs
- Informal tone

Tone must feel:
- Empowering
- Structured
- Inclusive
- Leadership-oriented

---

# 7. SUCCESS CRITERIA

The design is successful if:

- The mission is understood within 5 seconds.
- The layout feels structured and professional.
- The identity (women–LGBTQ++ leadership) is clear.
- Call-to-action buttons are visible without confusion.
- Branding is consistent throughout.

---

# 8. RESTRICTIONS

- No decorative clutter.
- No inconsistent color usage.
- No overly complex animations.
- No excessive text blocks.
- No weak or generic headlines.
